Building Bridges for International Students in Bridgeport, CT

My Mission Since 2021

I moved from India to the US to serve. Having navigated the stress and loneliness of a new country myself, I am passionate about helping international students at the University of Bridgeport find their identity and purpose in Christ.

"The Lord has been my strength... my mission is to help others experience that same hope."

🚗

Practical Support

We show Christ's love through grocery trips, driving lessons, and English help. We meet practical needs to build a foundation of trust.

🏠

Genuine Community

From sledding trips and karaoke to museum visits, we create a "home away from home" where students become family.

📖

Spiritual Growth

We provide safe spaces like Discovery Bible Studies for curious students to explore the love and truth of Jesus for themselves.

Who are Bridges & ICC?

Bridges International: A global community serving international students through service, friendship, and leadership development.

ICC (International Campus Church): Our local church partner in Bridgeport. It is a multi-national family where students lead worship and grow together.

When God Sends Snow, We Go Sledding! ❄️
