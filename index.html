About My Mission

Since 2021, I have had the privilege of serving as a Ministry Representative with International Students Inc. (ISI). My heart is for the students at the University of Bridgeport, helping them find a "home away from home" through our Bridges community.

We welcome students from all nations, offering friendship, practical help, and the opportunity to experience the love of Jesus during their time in the States.

When God Sends Snow, We Go Sledding! ❄️
