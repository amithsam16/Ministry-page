Amith & Nicole

ISI Ministry Representative & Volunteer | Bridgeport, CT

"We are passionate about helping international students find their identity and purpose in Christ through holistic care."

Serving with ISI at the University of Bridgeport, we are dedicated to creating a space where every student feels seen and supported. Amith serves as a full-time Ministry Representative, and Nicole partners as a volunteer to welcome students from every nation. Driven by the love of Christ, we seek to meet the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of those far from home.

🤝

Meet New Friends

We want you to have a place you can belong! Together, we host gatherings, fun events, and social opportunities to connect you with fellow students and the local community.

🚗

Practical Support

Whether you need someone to practice English with, a ride to the store, or help navigating Connecticut, we are here for you. Our goal is to see you thrive during your studies!

✝️

Spiritual Growth

Explore life’s biggest questions in a safe, welcoming environment. We offer resources and regular Bible discussions to help you discover God’s wisdom for your life.

Bridges & ICC

Bridges International: Our community for service and leadership development. We work to provide a "home away from home" for international students.

ICC (International Campus Church): A weekly student-led church service where we worship and grow together as a multi-national family of faith.
