❄️ When God Sends Snow, We Go Sledding!

A few weeks ago, a massive snowstorm shut down the city. We grabbed sleds and headed to the hills! For many students from Vietnam, India, and Egypt, it was their first time ever seeing snow. Watching their faces go from unsure to flying down the hill laughing was the highlight of my semester.

"Ministry doesn’t always have to be programmed. It can be as simple as sharing a snow day with people who are becoming family."
