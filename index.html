My Testimony

I moved from India to serve international students. My mission is to help others experience the hope and transformation I found in Christ through every trial and triumph.

What is Bridges?

Bridges International is a global community that welcomes and serves the million-plus international students in the U.S. We create a "home away from home" through service, friendship, and leadership development.

What is ICC?

International Campus Church (ICC) is our multi-national church family in Bridgeport. It is a place where students lead worship, serve, and grow together in their faith journey.
