"The Lord has been my strength and guide through every trial... My life’s mission is to help others experience the same hope."

I was born and raised in India. Today, I serve as a Ministry Representative with ISI at the University of Bridgeport. I am passionate about helping international students find their identity and purpose in Christ.

🤝

Meet New Friends

It can be hard to meet people when you arrive in the US, but we want you to have a place you can belong! We have regular gatherings, fun events like hiking and painting, and many opportunities to connect.

🚗

Practical Support

We want you to thrive! Whether you need someone to practice English with, a ride to the store, or someone to show you around Connecticut, we are here to help. Contact us if you have a need!

✝️

Spiritual Growth

Becoming an international student is a perfect chance to discover new things about life and faith. We offer resources on spiritual questions and regular times to discuss topics from the Bible about God and life.

Who are Bridges & ICC?

Bridges International: A global community dedicated to service, friendship, and leadership development for international students.

ICC (International Campus Church): A weekly church service run by international students, for international students. Join us to find worship, community, or just to see what a church is like!

Partner With the Mission

Above all, I ask for your prayers as I serve the international student community in Bridgeport. Prayer is my first priority and the greatest gift you can offer, as we trust God to open hearts and provide spiritual transformation.

Financial support is also a significant help in sustaining this local ministry. Your contributions allow us to provide essential meals, groceries, and transportation for students, showing them the love of Christ through practical service.
